OUR MISSION TOGETHER

Improve the quality and accessibility of healthcare through thoughtfulness, innovation, and hands-on community collaboration.

The Warren Alpert Medical School of Brown University

Brown's Alpert Medical School provides access to large, modern spaces in which allow for intense collaborations at the white board, amongst people, and for prototyping. Its location near the Providence healthcare community, including hospitals in Lifespan and Care New England, as well as the Providence Veterans Affairs Medical Center, allows for participants to actively engage in observations and discussions to identify important healthcare needs. This will allow for participants of Brown Hack Health to create Providence's most demanded healthcare solutions!
