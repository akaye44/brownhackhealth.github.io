Brown Hack Health


Brown Hack Health's hackathon: (n) an intensive 24-hour marathon weekend collaboration, creating innovative solutions to current healthcare problems, and involving students, patients, venture capitalists, engineers, designers, IT developers, and healthcare professionals

Fri-Sun. September 17-19, 2016.
Warren Alpert Medical School, Brown University!

MORE INFO

Hosted at Brown's Medical School

Brown's Alpert Medical School provides access to large, modern spaces in which allow for intense collaborations at the white board, amongst people, and for prototyping. Its location near the Rhode Island Hospital and the healthcare community in Providence allows for participants to actively engage in observations and discussions to identify important healthcare needs. This will allow for participants of Brown Hack Health to create Providence's most demanded healthcare solutions!
